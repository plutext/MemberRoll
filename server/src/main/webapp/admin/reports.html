Reports

Cross-cutting exports — each downloads a CSV to open in a spreadsheet. The per-year exports (AGM register, mailing labels, financial) stay on the Renewals page where the year's work happens; links below.

Register of members

The constitution's clause 4 register: every person who holds or has ever held a formal membership place — full name, one address (preferred postal, else email), the date they became a member and, for former members, the date they ceased. Unlike the AGM register it includes lapsed and ceased members: that is the point. Known limitation: for members brought in by the spreadsheet import, "date became a member" is the start of the earliest imported year, not their true historical join date.

People without a current membership

Current household people (not left, not deceased) holding no membership place in the chosen year — who fell through the cracks at rollover, person by person, with the last year they did hold a place (blank = never).

Unrenewed households

Households financial (Paid) in the first year whose state in the second is anything else — unpaid, lapsed, ceased, or no membership at all ("—"). The ring-around chase list, with the primary contact's email and phone.

Donations

Payments carrying a donation part, by received date (leave the dates blank for all time), with a trailing total. Reversals appear as negative rows — the export sums to the ledger.

Other exports

  • Renewals page — AGM register, mailing labels and financial CSVs for the selected period, and the reconciliation / Xero-journal exports for the treasurer.
