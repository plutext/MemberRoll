Committee

The society's office-bearers and ordinary committee members (constitution cl. 14–17). A committee position is a term-bounded appointment, AGM to AGM: recording an AGM slate ends every current appointment and opens the new ones. Corrections are edits, not reversals — fix a mis-typed office in place, or remove a bogus row.
